# Awesome Electronics

Curated list of awesome electronics tools, libraries, and resources.


## Books

- [The Art of Electronics] - Comprehensive reference by Horowitz and Hill.
- [Make: Electronics] - Hands-on introduction by Charles Platt.
- [Practical Electronics for Inventors] - Broad reference by Paul Scherz and Simon Monk.
- [Designing Analog Chips] - Free book on analog IC design by Hans Camenzind.

[The Art of Electronics]: https://artofelectronics.net/
[Make: Electronics]: https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/make-electronics-3rd/9781680456868/
[Practical Electronics for Inventors]: https://www.mheducation.com/highered/mhp/product/practical-electronics-inventors-fourth-edition.html
[Designing Analog Chips]: http://www.designinganalogchips.com


## Learning

- [All About Circuits] - Textbooks, tutorials, and a large community forum.
- [Electronics Tutorials] - Tutorials covering DC, AC, semiconductors, and logic.
- [Khan Academy: Electrical Engineering] - Free video courses on circuit analysis.
- [MIT OpenCourseWare: Circuits and Electronics] - Full university course materials.
- [Ben Eater] - Build-from-scratch tutorials for CPUs and digital logic.

[All About Circuits]: https://www.allaboutcircuits.com
[Electronics Tutorials]: https://www.electronics-tutorials.ws
[Khan Academy: Electrical Engineering]: https://www.khanacademy.org/science/electrical-engineering
[MIT OpenCourseWare: Circuits and Electronics]: https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-002-circuits-and-electronics-spring-2007/
[Ben Eater]: https://eater.net


## EDA & Simulation

- [KiCad] - Open-source schematic capture and PCB layout.
- [LTspice] - Free SPICE simulator from Analog Devices.
- [ngspice] - Open-source mixed-signal circuit simulator.
- [Falstad Circuit Simulator] - Interactive in-browser circuit simulator.
- [CircuitJS] - Open-source fork of the Falstad simulator.
- [EasyEDA] - Browser-based schematic, simulation, and PCB layout.
- [Fritzing] - Beginner-friendly schematic, breadboard, and PCB tool.
- [Horizon EDA] - Modern open-source EDA suite.
- [Qucs] - Open-source integrated circuit simulator.

[KiCad]: https://www.kicad.org
[LTspice]: https://www.analog.com/en/resources/design-tools-and-calculators/ltspice-simulator.html
[ngspice]: https://ngspice.sourceforge.io
[Falstad Circuit Simulator]: https://www.falstad.com/circuit/
[CircuitJS]: https://github.com/pfalstad/circuitjs1
[EasyEDA]: https://easyeda.com
[Fritzing]: https://fritzing.org
[Horizon EDA]: https://horizon-eda.org
[Qucs]: https://qucs.sourceforge.net/


## Hardware Platforms

- [Arduino] - Microcontroller boards and IDE for hobbyist projects.
- [Raspberry Pi] - Single-board computers for embedded and Linux projects.
- [Adafruit] - Boards, breakouts, and excellent learning guides.
- [SparkFun] - Boards, sensors, and tutorials.
- [STM32] - ARM Cortex-M microcontrollers from STMicroelectronics.
- [ESP32] - Wi-Fi and Bluetooth SoCs from Espressif.

[Arduino]: https://www.arduino.cc
[Raspberry Pi]: https://www.raspberrypi.com
[Adafruit]: https://www.adafruit.com
[SparkFun]: https://www.sparkfun.com
[STM32]: https://www.st.com/en/microcontrollers-microprocessors/stm32-32-bit-arm-cortex-mcus.html
[ESP32]: https://www.espressif.com/en/products/socs/esp32


## Components & Sourcing

- [Digi-Key] - Large component distributor with detailed parametric search.
- [Mouser] - Component distributor with deep stock.
- [LCSC] - Low-cost component distributor, often paired with JLCPCB.
- [Octopart] - Cross-distributor search engine for parts and datasheets.
- [Findchips] - Component price and availability aggregator.

[Digi-Key]: https://www.digikey.com
[Mouser]: https://www.mouser.com
[LCSC]: https://www.lcsc.com
[Octopart]: https://octopart.com
[Findchips]: https://www.findchips.com


## PCB Fabrication

- [JLCPCB] - Low-cost PCB fab and assembly service.
- [PCBWay] - PCB fab, assembly, and prototyping.

- [Aisler] - EU-based PCB fab with KiCad integration.
